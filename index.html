About us
Beauty parlor
We strive to enhance your natural beauty with every visit.

Founded back in 1999, Beauty Parlor has been the premier salon in New York City. Our work was featured in a few most popular magazines such as Vogue, Elle, W, Marie Claire, and Allure! Beauty Parlor is focused on the latest trends in hair and fashion! Every week our professional stylists undergo training courses by most famous educators and even go overseas to attend the most advanced beauty treatment master classes!

Beauty Parlor offers a full range of salon treatments and beauty services provided by a team of professional stylists, all in a clean and welcoming environment. We practice the art of beauty and offer modern hair styling, coloring, and cutting services for men and women at very affordable prices.
